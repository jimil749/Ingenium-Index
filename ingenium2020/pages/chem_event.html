Need For Solution

Do you think you have what it takes to deal with real-time industrial problems? This competition will give you the chance to apply your chemical engineering prowess. The day of competition would be the deadline where you would present the solution you come up with, using the skills you’ve gathered. It would be a great way to set off your practical learning experience to build a career in the chemical industry!
