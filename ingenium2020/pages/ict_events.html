Codefeeder

Codefeeder is one of our quirkier events, just your basic coding competition with some not-so-basic rules. It is basically a timed relay. Form a team of 3 people. All get to code, but in a manner that no two members get to interact with each other. Divided into 4 slots of 15 minutes each, every participant gets one slot. The final half-hour comes as a bonus slot which can be used as the team wishes. The trick is to code quick, efficient and in sync!

DATE : 21st March 2020, 3:00pm

A nail biting competition among the teams to flex their finesse in competitive coding.
