    About Us
    The Ingenious Mechathon is a 36-hour competition where teams compete to formulate the most creative and practical industrial solutions to mechanical problems. The problems are of a wide range and designed while keeping in mind an industry-oriented approach, with support from many partner individuals such as faculty and alumni working in the field.

    Conducted as a part of Ingenium, this event is set to be held for the first time in Gujarat. Every team works on the selected problem and is evaluated for their progress, innovation, and the practicality of their work, on regular intervals. The problem statements will be released one week prior to the Mechathon along with the details of the expected deliverables; such as software simulations or prototyping. All problem statements will be comprehensive and declaring what the end product should achieve.
